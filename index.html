CEDAR's Mission

Our mission is to facilitate the increase of knowledge and understanding of the many benefits the Coal Industry provides in our daily lives by providing financial resources and coal education materials to implement its study in the school curriculum.
We also provide opportunity, incentive, and encouragement to teachers and students to become problem solvers relative to either/or both of the following:

  • Investigate and identify the causes of the significant decline in the demand for coal as an energy source, and explore possible solutions to those causes, or possible new uses for coal, that could enable the industry to regain its prominence as a thriving industry within our region;
  • Investigate and research ways to re-purpose abandoned mined lands, and/or reclaimed mined lands that could help provide economic stimulation to our region.
