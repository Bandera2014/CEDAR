About CEDAR

Our History

We are a not-for-profit corporation was formed in July 19993 as a partnership between the Coal Industry, Business Community and Academia. The organization was formed through the joint efforts of the North Carolina Coal Institute (NCCI-The Coal Institute) and Coal Operators and Associates of Pikeville, Kentucky for the purpose of improving the image of the Coal Industry.
CEDAR West KY started in 1996 and covers 6 counties.
