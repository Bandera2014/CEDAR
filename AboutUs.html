About CEDAR

Our History

CEDAR is a not-for-profit 501 C 3 organization that began in July of 1993 in Pikeville, KY. The organization was formed through the joint efforts of the North Carolina Coal Institute and Coal Operators and Associates of Pikeville. CEDAR expanded its reach into West KY in 1996 when CEDAR West KY was formed and again in 2018 when CEDAR Indiana began.
